Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our programs, memberships, sessions, and how everything works at IHS.

Support

Everything You Need to Know

What is Innovator HuzSam?
Innovator HuzSam is an organization that helps people achieve their goals through practical, real-world training and guidance. We train learners globally with the aim of education, progress, and prosperity for everyone, regardless of background.
What courses does Innovator HuzSam offer?
We offer training in languages, communication skills, digital skills, career development, personal development, and other practical programs designed for real-world use.
Are all trainings conducted online?
Yes, all trainings are delivered online, allowing flexible learning from anywhere.
Are the classes live or recorded?
Our programs include live interactive classes, along with supporting materials and guided practice. Recorded lectures and trainings may also be provided on demand, depending on the program.
Who can join your training programs?
Anyone can join our training programs. Our courses are suitable for students, professionals, and individuals seeking personal or professional growth. There are no age, gender, qualification, or country restrictions.
How can I get started?
You can get started by choosing a membership plan or contacting us through the Contact page for guidance. You may also reach us on WhatsApp for live support.
How can I enroll or request more information?
You can contact us through the Contact page to request details or guidance. You may also reach us on WhatsApp for live support.
What levels do you offer for language and skill training?
We offer training from basic to advanced levels, based on the learner’s needs.
Do you offer spoken and written language training?
Yes, language programs include both spoken and written training for personal and professional use.
Are your courses suitable for beginners?
Yes, our programs are designed for beginners, intermediate, and advanced learners.
Do you provide customized or one-to-one training?
Yes, we offer personalized training, including one-to-one and small group sessions.
Do you offer training for specific skills or subjects on request?
Yes, we provide customized training based on client-requested skills, subjects, or goals.
How long does a training program take?
The duration depends on the program, learning level, and training format. Details are shared after assessment.
Do you offer career guidance and professional development support?
Yes, we provide career guidance, interview preparation, CV optimization, and workplace communication training.
Are counselling and wellbeing services confidential?
Yes, all counselling and wellbeing services are strictly confidential.
