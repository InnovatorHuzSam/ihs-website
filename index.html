Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our programs, membership, and how everything works.

What is Innovator HuzSam (IHS)?
Innovator HuzSam (IHS) is a global online education and training organization. We offer structured programs in languages, digital skills, career development, counselling, and more — all designed around practical learning and long-term growth.
How do I get started with IHS?
Getting started is simple. Browse our services to find a program that suits your goals, choose a membership plan, and reach out to us via the contact form or WhatsApp. We'll guide you through the enrollment process.
Are sessions conducted online?
Yes, all our training sessions are conducted online. This allows learners from around the world to access our programs from the comfort of their homes, with flexible scheduling options to suit different time zones.
What languages do you teach?
We offer training in English, Urdu, Hindi, Arabic, French, Bengali, and Chinese. We also provide exam preparation for IELTS, TOEFL, and PTE.
Can I book a free trial session?
Yes! We offer a free trial session so you can experience our teaching style and discuss your learning goals before committing. Simply fill out the contact form or message us on WhatsApp to arrange yours.
Do you offer group or corporate training?
Yes, we provide customized group and corporate training programs. Whether you need team-based language courses or organizational development workshops, we can tailor a program to meet your requirements.
