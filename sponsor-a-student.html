Sponsor a Student

Help someone access quality education and training who may not be able to afford it on their own. Every contribution creates a real opportunity.

Supporting Access to Learning

Why Sponsorship Matters

Education has the power to change lives — but not everyone has equal access to it. Many talented, motivated individuals are unable to pursue training because of financial barriers.

Through our Sponsor a Student initiative, you can directly support a learner's education by funding their training program. Whether it's a language course, digital skills training, or career development program, your sponsorship provides them with the tools they need to grow.

Every sponsorship is handled transparently. You'll know exactly how your contribution is being used and the impact it creates.

Your Sponsorship Can Provide

  • Full or partial training program fees
  • Access to professional, qualified trainers
  • Learning materials and resources
  • Session recordings and study support
  • A certificate of completion for the learner
How It Works

How Sponsorship Works

Our sponsorship process is straightforward, transparent, and designed to create maximum impact for the sponsored learner.

Express Your Interest

Contact us via the form below, email, or WhatsApp to let us know you'd like to sponsor a student. We'll discuss your preferences and any specific areas you'd like to support.

We Match You with a Learner

Based on your preferences, we identify a learner who would benefit from the sponsored training. We'll share relevant details (with the learner's consent) so you understand who you're supporting.

Training Begins

The sponsored learner is enrolled in the appropriate program with a dedicated trainer. They receive the same quality of training as any other IHS member.

Progress Updates

We provide you with regular updates on the learner's progress, including milestones achieved and the impact of your sponsorship. Full transparency throughout the journey.

Sponsorship Options

You can choose how you'd like to contribute:

Full Sponsorship

Cover the complete training program fees for one learner.

Partial Sponsorship

Contribute a portion of the fees — we'll match or pool contributions to cover the rest.

Recurring Sponsorship

Set up ongoing support to continuously sponsor learners over time.

The Impact of Your Generosity
